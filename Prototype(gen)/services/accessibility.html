Accessibility Services

Troy Public Library aims to make library spaces, resources, and services more accessible for all visitors.

Accessible Support

Library staff can help visitors locate materials, use public services, and find resources that support different access needs.

If you need assistance during your visit, please ask at the service desk.

Planning Your Visit

If you have questions before arriving, contact the library in advance so staff can help you prepare for your visit and answer service questions.

Building Access

Find information about entering the building and getting assistance once you arrive.

Help with Technology

Staff may be able to assist with computers, printing, and accessing library resources.
