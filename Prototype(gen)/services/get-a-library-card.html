Services

Get a Library Card

Sign up for a Troy Public Library card to borrow materials, use online resources, and access library services.

Who Can Get a Card?

Library cards are available to local residents and eligible users who want access to Troy Public Library collections and services.

Please bring a photo ID and proof of your current address when applying in person.

What You’ll Need

Bring identification such as:

  • Photo ID
  • Proof of current address
  • Parent or guardian information for children, if needed

Borrow Books & Media

Use your card to check out books, DVDs, and other library materials.

Access Digital Resources

Use your card for online collections, databases, and downloadable content.

Manage Your Account

Renew items, place holds, and check due dates through your library account.

How to Apply

You can visit the library to apply for a card and speak with staff if you have questions about eligibility or required documents.

Contact the Library

Need Help?

If you are unsure what documents to bring or whether you already have an account, library staff can help you get started.
