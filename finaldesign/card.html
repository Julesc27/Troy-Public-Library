Get a Library Card

Free for all New York State residents. Takes just a few minutes.

Home  ›  Services  ›  Get a Library Card

✨  Apply for a library card online

Fill out a quick form and pick up your card at the library.

Getting a Library Card

Cards will be issued in person to any resident of New York State who can provide two forms of identification, one of which includes your current address.

Valid forms of identification include:
🪪 Driver's license or state-issued non-driver's ID with your current address
🏷️ ID card or badge with your name typed (NOT written) on it
📬 Mail with your name and current address, postmarked within the past 30 days
Don't have these forms of ID?

Check at the Check Out desk to see what else you can use. If necessary, a postcard can be sent to your address for verification.

For Your Information

🔄
One card across 29 libraries
You may not hold more than one valid library card from any of the 29 libraries in the Upper Hudson Library System. All cards are interchangeable between member libraries.
💳
Replacement cards cost $1.00
If necessary you can replace your card for $1.00, but you must show a picture ID to do so.
🔒
Lost or stolen card
Let us know immediately. You are responsible for all materials charged on your card by anyone.
📝
Keep your info up to date
Inform us of any name, telephone, or address change as soon as it occurs.
🗺️
Troy Public Library is part of the Upper Hudson Library System.
Your Troy Public Library card is accepted at most public libraries in Rensselaer and Albany counties.
