Design rationale

Instead of a bright, flat, modern aesthetic, this version uses deep burgundy, forest green, antique gold, and parchment backgrounds to create a more historic and cultural feel. The goal is to feel closer to a museum, archive, or reading room while still being approachable.

Accessibility priorities

Large headings, clear contrast, easy scanning, consistent buttons, and simple top-level categories keep the experience intuitive for first-time visitors, families, and older patrons.
